Welcome to Surrey Explorer

Surrey is known as the most wooded county in England, with stunning areas to go walking and exploring such as the Surrey Hills Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, Winkworth Arboretum and Box Hill. There are lots of places of interest to visit like Hampton Court Palace and Polesden Lacey. Why not make it a weekend away and stay in one of the many hotels in the county.

When you have browsed the places on Google Maps simply tick the box next to the selection and start to build your itinerary.
